Spend Small Save Big

Buying food in bulk can be an easy way to save more money while spending less, My favourite way to do this is stock up on Meats like chicken and prep it before freezing, I usually buy 2 or 3 Large Chicken breasts and to prep it for a one person meal i cut the breast in half and dice before transferring into snaplock bags to freeze. This not only saves you money but when it comes time to cook half the work has already been done!

Prepping food into serving size bags makes cooking a lot easier, it is much cheaper to self portion your meals, and food, such as chips for examples, it is far cheaper to buy a full size bag of chips and portion them in bags or containers for lunch yourself, By portioning your food yourself you are creating less plastic waste and saving money, both benefiting you and the environment. There's really no reason why you shouldn't do it now.
